Analyzing the impact of regional logistics delays on customer churn rates using the Olist E-Commerce dataset (Excel/Power Pivot).

# Customer Retention Optimization: Analyzing the Cost of Regional Logistics Delays on Churn Risk

## 1. Project Background (The Context)
Olist operates in Brazil, a country with massive geographical dimensions where shipping distances pose a significant logistical challenge. Delivery delays are common, but the impact on customer satisfaction worsens significantly when these delays are paired with **high shipping costs (freight value)**. 

When customers pay a premium for shipping but receive their packages later than the estimated SLA (Service Level Agreement), their trust drops. This negative experience often leads to customer churn—where they abandon the platform for competitors. This project analyzes the intersection of spatial logistics, shipping costs, and their combined effect on customer purchasing behavior.

## 2. Key Stakeholders
The insights generated from this dashboard are designed to support strategic decisions for two primary groups:
* **VP of Operations / Supply Chain:** To identify geographical bottlenecks, evaluate regional courier performance, and optimize delivery routes.
* **VP of Marketing / CRM:** To quantify the churn risk, calculate lost revenue, and design targeted retention campaigns (e.g., shipping vouchers) for high-risk customer segments.

## 3. Core Business Questions
This analysis is driven by the following critical questions:
1. **Spatial & Logistics Performance:** Which Brazilian states experience the highest frequency of delivery delays and the highest average shipping costs?
2. **Customer Choice & Behavior Analysis:** How does the combination of missed delivery SLAs and above-average shipping costs increase the probability of customer churn? 
3. **Financial Impact:** What is the estimated potential revenue loss (based on average order value) attributed to customers who churned specifically due to poor logistics experiences?
